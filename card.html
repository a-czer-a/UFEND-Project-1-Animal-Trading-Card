Dachshund

Dachshunds have traditionally been viewed as a symbol of Germany.

  • Common nicknames: Teckel, Sausage Dog
  • Average length: 17–25 cm
  • Average lifespan: 13 years
  • Origin: Germany

A typical dachshund is long-bodied and muscular with short, stubby legs. Dachshunds are playful, but as hunting dogs can be quite stubborn, and are known for their propensity for chasing small animals, birds, and tennis balls with great determination and ferocity.
